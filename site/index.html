FAQ.Engaging with hostelities

How do I use the Hostel Washing Machine?

Book a convenient slot for using the washing machine by writing your name and phone number in the register near the security desk before 8 pm for the following day. Ask for the key to the laundry room at the security desk during the time slot booked. Lock the door before leaving and return the key to the security desk.

Where is the laundry room?

The laundry room is to the north eastern corner of the building adjacent to the sports room.

How do I issue Sport equipments?

Visit the security desk with either a permanent or a temporary ID card(if permanent ID cards are not yet issued) and ask the security personnel for the required sport equipment.Submit your ID card and sign in the register before taking possesion of the equipments and return it later for recieving back the ID card.

SIANG HOSTEL-A BRIEF INTRO

Siang hostel came into being on July 2003 as the fourth boy's hostel of IIT, Guwahati. The name 'Siang' follows the same revered tradition of naming hostels of IIT Guwahati after the tributaries of the river Brahmaputra. The home of some of the brightest students from various programmes and branches (B.Tech), Siang is reputed for its peaceful and friendly environment. The hostel building overlooks the majestic cricket ground, and with its close proximity to the Academic block, Siang is one of the best places you can proudly call 'home'. A splendid green hill and a lake with chirping birds adds elegance to the natural beauty of this serene hostel, especially during sunsets.
